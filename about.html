More about me

After almost a year and a half in this company, I decided to take some time off and find a more fullfilling job. I had not realized then that coding was the path I would take a few months later.

I'm now a recent graduate from the SheCodes program teaching Front end programming to women.

As a frontend developer I can build websites or web applications.
